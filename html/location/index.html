Kent Manor Inn

The 1820 Historic Kent Manor Inn is located at 500 Kent Manor Drive on Kent Island in Stevensville Maryland.

The Inn and glass-enclosed Garden House sit on 220 acres of woods and farmland, including a mile of waterfront on the shore of the Thompson Creek.

The Inn and area hotels are about a 35-minute drive from BWI Airport and BWI Amtrak Rail Station. Rental cars are available at BWI Airport.

If you are driving directly to the wedding Friday evening, we recommend that you check the traffic reports early as traffic over the Bay Bridge on Friday evening could be significantly backed up.
