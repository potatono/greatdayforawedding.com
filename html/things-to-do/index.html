Golf Courses

Hog Neck Golf Course

Hog Neck Golf Course, located in Easton, Maryland, features an 18-hole Championship Talbot County golf course as well as 9-hole Executive course ready to test your golfing skills. In addition to our 27 holes of golf, we also offer complete learning areas to hone your game. These areas include an extremely large putting green, a newly designed chipping green complex, and a driving range with both grass and mat hitting areas.

The Hog Neck Golf Course was just rated the "Finest Golf for 2012, on Maryland's Upper Eastern Shore" by Maryland Life Magazine's Readers Poll.

Queenstown Harbor Golf Course

Welcome to Queenstown Harbor! Winner of the 2012 Environmental Leader in Golf Award! One of the most scenic and challenging golf experiences located just outside Washington, D.C., Queenstown Harbor features 36 holes of championship golf on two unique layouts - The River and The Lakes. Spectacular Chesapeake Bay waterfront beauty and superior service make for an unforgettable round at Maryland's premier golf destination.

Boat Charters

Southern Bell Charters

Southern Belle Charters offers both full day and half day charters. Full day charters depart the docks at 6am and return to dock at 2pm. Half day charters offer two options; 6am departure with return at 11am or a 1pm departure returning at 6pm.

Lead Dog Charters

Lead Dog Charters is a full-time Chesapeake Bay, Maryland fishing and cruising charter boat operation available for bookings seven days a week from April through December.
